Wordtile is a place where users share text(like a forum). The name is a modern Anglicised blend of German roots: Wort meaning "word" and Teilen meaning "to share." It shows the idea of a word-part or the sharing of a word.

 

Pronounced WORT-ile (rhymes with "short mile"), IPA: /wɔːrˈtaɪl/, it has the German 'ei' sound for a unique sound.

 

Inspired by linguistic forms like participles and morphemes, Wordtile shows the beauty of language in motion, where all words are vital infomation. Infomation is the internet.

 

Wortile will be coded with HTML, CSS, JS and Python
